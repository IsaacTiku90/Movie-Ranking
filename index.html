The Best TV Shows According to Isaac

My top 3 TV-Shows of all-time.

Community

Community is a show about a group of misfits who attend a community college. The show is a comedy and is very funny. The show is very meta and has a lot of references to other shows and movies.


Barry

Barry is a show about a hitman who wants to become an actor. The show is a comedy and is very funny. The show is very meta and has a lot of references to other shows and movies.


Reno 911

Reno 911 is a show about a sheriff's department in Reno, Nevada. The show is a comedy and is very funny.
